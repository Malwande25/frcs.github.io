Welcome to Faith Revival Christian School!

Our vision is to instill in each child the fear of the Lord through Biblical education. We, thus, make it our main goal to develop a complete human being through academic excellence and character building.

At the heart of our school lies a commitment to personal character development, nurturing the young minds to be the best version of what God created them to be. We are privileged to be using a world-class, individualized, educational methodology, Accelerated Christian Education (ACE), that is innovative and well-designed to fully unlock each student’s academic potential.

Passionate and devoted teaching staff, fully engaging parents, and excited pupils all contribute to making our campus a desirable place to learn and grow. Our learners engage in various sporting and cultural events that create lifelong memories. We pride ourselves on maintaining a clean and safe environment in which each learner’s uniqueness is appreciated.

Situated on the shores of the warm Indian Ocean in East London, Faith Revival Christian School is committed to making schooling a memorable experience, producing caring individuals who are making an impact in the world by being the light and the salt the world desperately needs.

About Us

Faith Revival Christian School was established to provide a holistic education based on biblical principles. Our school stands out for its commitment to nurturing character, academic excellence, and spiritual growth. We empower students to excel in all aspects of life while fostering a deep respect for God's teachings.
