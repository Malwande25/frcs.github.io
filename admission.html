Application information and Dates

Our school uses an individualized curriculum called Accelerated Christian Education (ACE). Applicants for Grade 1 to 8 need to do a Diagnostic Test to a certain that they are ready for the grade they wish to apply for, and so that learning gaps can be identified and addressed if there are any. Grade RR and R need no test.

Applications for the upcoming school year must be submitted by the following dates:

  • Early Application Deadline: March 1, 2024
  • Regular Application Deadline: June 15, 2024
  • Late Application Deadline (if spaces are available): August 1, 2024
